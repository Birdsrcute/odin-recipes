Tortilla Egg Breakfast

Home

very easy egg breakfast idea that is delicious

Ingredients:

  • tortilla
  • 2 eggs
  • handful of spinach
  • handful of cheese
  • half a tomato
  • salt
  • black pepper seasoning
  • oil
  • optional: bacon pieces
  • optional: pepper red flakes

Steps:

  1. crack two eggs into a bowl and mix them together
  2. season the eggs with salt and black pepper
  3. prepare pan by adding oil and adding in sliced tomatoes first
  4. after a min, add in your egg mixture
  5. add in spinach and cheese on top
  6. season with more stuff if you want, like pepper flakes or bacon
  7. scrape the edges and add a tortilla on top after the egg has solidified
  8. flip the tortilla with the egg
  9. wait until the tortilla is crispy to bend it like a sandwich
  10. done

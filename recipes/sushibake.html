Sushi Bake

Home

This is a great sushi bake recipe from me personally, your welcome

Ingredients:

  • sushi rice
  • canned tuna
  • furikake
  • bagel seasoning
  • rice vinegar
  • kewpie mayonnaise
  • siracha sauce
  • sugar
  • salt
  • seaweed snacks
  • green onion
  • imitation crab
  • cream cheese
  • oil

Part 1: Preparing Sushi Rice

  1. Cook sushi rice
  2. Mix rice vinegar, sugar, and a pinch of salt: 1 cup of rice = 1 tbsp of equal parts vinegar and sugar
  3. Add in sushi rice to the mixture: keep folding instead of separating

Part 2: Preparing Crab/Tuna Mixture

  1. Defrost imitation crab and add shredded pieces into bowl
  2. Mix Crab and tuna in bowl
  3. Add in Kewpie Mayonnaise
  4. Add in a bit of cream cheese (optional)
  5. Add in a bit of sriracha
  6. Add in green onions
  7. Mix Crab and tuna in bowl
  8. Add in Kewpie Mayonnaise
  9. Add in a bit of cream cheese (optional)
  10. Add in a bit of sriracha

Part 3: Preparing Baking Pan

  1. Add in oil and rub excess with a paper towel to prevent sticking to pan
  2. Scoop rice into baking pan and press it into pan (until about half)
  3. Add in bagel seasoning/furikake (or crush some seaweeds sheets on top)
  4. Add in a bit of cream cheese (optional)
  5. Top it off with crab/tuna mixture
  6. Add in more mayo and more bagel seasoning on top (optional)

Part 4: Cooking

  1. Preheat 400 degree oven and keep it in for 20 minutes (half bake half broil)
  2. Serve hot with seaweed sheets

Paneer Bhurji

Home

very easy and delicious vegetarian North Indian dish made with crumbled paneer

Ingredients:

  • 375g paneer, crumbled
  • 2 large onions, finely chopped
  • 1 large tomato, finely chopped
  • 5 green chillies, finely chopped (optional, less for less spice)
  • 1 tbsp grated ginger
  • 5 cloves garlic
  • 2 tbsp besan or 1 tbsp cornstarch
  • 3 tbsp yogurt
  • 1 tsp salt
  • 1/2 tsp turmeric powder
  • 1 tsp cumin powder
  • 1 tsp coriander powder
  • 1/2 tsp red chili powder
  • 1/2 tsp garam masala
  • 2 tbsp oil or butter
  • handful of cilantro, chopped

Prep the Veggies and Spice Paste

  1. Dice 2 large red onions and 1 large tomato.
  2. Chop up a handful of cilantro and 5 green chillies.
  3. Mince 5 cloves of garlic and grate 1 tbsp of ginger (if you do not have ginger paste)
  4. mix all the spices in a bowl and set aside
  5. Add in a bit of sriracha
  6. Add in green onions
  7. Mix Crab and tuna in bowl
  8. Add in Kewpie Mayonnaise
  9. Add in a bit of cream cheese (optional)
  10. Add in a bit of sriracha

Actually Cooking Stuff

  1. heat oil in pan and add chopped onions, saute until golden
  2. add ginger garlic paste and green chilies. cook for 1 minute
  3. add 1 chopped tomato and cook until soft and mushy
  4. stir in the prepared spice paste. Cook for 2 minutes until the oil separates.
  5. crumble paneer into pan. mix well and cook for 5 minutes until its coated and creamy
  6. turn off the heat. add chopped cilantro, mix, and serve hot
